About Us

It has been over 13 years since Cafe and Bakery first inception in 2008.

With Leandra's insistence on quality, hard-work, consistency and customer service inherited by 2 generations has been their perfect recipe for success.

Their love, experience, and knowledge have made the Cabalonga family bakery the most successful and well-known bakery in Ilagan City offering the finest baked and brewed good in the whole Ilagan City area.
